RgA Rules — Recreational Golfers of America

RgA Rules promote speedy play, they eliminate unfortunate lies, and they make golf a little bit easier and MUCH MORE fun.

  • Handicaps — World Handicap System (WHS), updated after each posted round
  • Scoring — Stroke play, net score against par, full handicap applied
  • Out of Bounds — Stroke & distance unless preferred-lie rule is active
  • Lost Ball — 2-stroke penalty, drop in approximate area of loss
  • Pace of Play — 40 seconds maximum per shot once it is your turn
  • Mulligans — One per nine holes, tee shots only, declared before teeing
  • Gimme Putts — Within the leather, by agreement of playing partners
  • Preferred Lies — When active, ball may be moved within one club length, no closer to hole

Trip History & Info

The McKees Rocks MASTERS Golf Tournament is played every Spring in the Myrtle Beach, SC area. The annual trip brings together the RgA group for several days of competitive and recreational golf.

The trip format includes stroke play, team events, a skins game, and the Donnie Coultas Memorial Round. Final standings are posted in Trip Archives after each trip concludes.
